# ANSI Escape Sequences

## Graphics Mode
Format: \x1b[<VALUE>;...;<VALUE>m
| Value |      Attribute     |
|-------|:-------------------|
|0      |All attributes off  |
|1      |Bold On             |
|2      |Faded On            |
|3      |Italics On          |
|4      |Underscore On       |
|5      |Blink On            |
|7      |Reverse Video On    |
|8      |Concealed On        |
